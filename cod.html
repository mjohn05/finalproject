Call of Duty: Black Ops 3

Call of Duty: Black Ops III is a first-person shooter video game, developed by Treyarch and published by Activision. It is the twelfth entry in the Call of Duty series and the sequel to the 2012 video game Call of Duty: Black Ops II. It was released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One on November 6, 2015.







Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare Remastered is a first-person shooter video game developed by Raven Software and published by Activision. It is a remastered version of Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare, a game that was released in November 2007 for PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and Microsoft Windows. The remastered version was released worldwide on November 4, 2016 for PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Microsoft Windows.
